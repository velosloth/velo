Microsoft Intune

Active Directory in the Cloud

January 9, 2026

If you already have your Active Directory domain setup, why even bother moving to AzureAD or Hybrid AD?

With users working from home, BYoD and the plethora of mobile devices under our umbrellas can become daunting to manage. AzureAD and Intune can help tremendously with security, maintenance and accounting of our systems.

Intune Joined Windows Systems

If you're Windows clients are joined to your domain via Microsoft Intune, you have just as much control, if not more control over these systems, compared to Active Directory. Your Group Policy Objects can still be applied via Intune, as well as pushing updates (replace WSUS), pushing application installs & uninstalls, remote lock & wipe options, Antivirus monitoring, the list goes on.
